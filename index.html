Introduction

Melbourne is globally celebrated as a premier coffee capital, built on a vibrant ecosystem of independent operators. This visualization explores how the city's unique hospitality footprint shifts across different neighborhoods, analyzing seating dynamics and geographic densities.

1. Geographic Distribution of Cafés

This map layers the locations of all dining and café establishments across municipality boundaries, revealing dense commercial clusters.

2. Café Counts by Neighborhood Area

A neighborhood breakdown identifying which local areas serve as the heaviest hubs for café culture.

3. Seating Dynamics: Indoor vs. Outdoor

An exploration of spatial layouts, tracking how businesses distribute their capacity between indoor dining and al fresco street seating.
